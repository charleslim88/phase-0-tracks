Elevator Directions for a Robot

-Wait for the elevator door to open:
	-Until you hear a bell or until the door opens:
		-Wait patiently
	-If elevator door does not open:
		-Then press the up or down button again
	-If elevator door opens
		-Then walk inside
-Decide which floor you want to go to:
	-When you figure it out
		-Then press the lighted number that corresponds to the floor you want to go
			-Wait for the elevator to reach the desired floor:
				-Then walk out when door opens
	- If elevator door opens on the wrong floor:
		-Then stay inside the elevator and press the desired floor number again
	-If elevator door opens on the correct floor:
		-Then walk outside of the elevator
